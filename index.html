About Interface

Interface is a nexus of technology, innovation and art organised by the department of COMPUTER SCIENCE hosted on August 20 and 21, 2019. The two day extravaganza witnesses the coalescence of the final exponents of technical acumen as hundreds and thousands compete in series of events in a bid to emerge victorious. Interface has successfully surfaced as a brand in itself, invoking participation from thousands of students from different institutions and universities across India. It provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills vis-a-vis real life technological scenarios that challenge them to think on the go and apply everything they have learned so far.
